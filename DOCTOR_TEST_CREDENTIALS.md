## Doctor Dashboard Features

Once logged in, you'll see:

- **Dashboard** - Overview of today's appointments & metrics
- **My Schedule** - View and manage your appointment calendar
- **Live Queue** - Real-time patient queue with wait times
- **Appointments** - Full appointment history
- **Patient Records** - Access to patient medical information
- **Prescriptions** - Manage patient prescriptions
- **Analytics** - Queue performance, utilization metrics
- **Settings** - Profile and availability management
